Why Tamil?

Tamil has one of the world's oldest continuous literary traditions. For centuries, words carried identity, place, values, and memory. En Peyar brings that tradition into modern naming.

Inspired by Tamil words, history, literature, and sound. Not trends.

Before code is written or capital is raised, naming is the first act of creation. It shapes your team's focus, defines your vision, and lays the absolute foundation of your brand identity.
